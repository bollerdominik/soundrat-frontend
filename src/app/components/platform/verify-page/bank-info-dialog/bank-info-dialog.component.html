How to receive payments to your bank

We use the established payment provider Stripe to securely send payments from your fans to your bank account


  • 1. Click the "Receive payments to your bank" button and create an account with Stripe
  • 2. If asked what type of Business you are select "Individual" (Unless you already own a different type of Business)
  • 3. Select "Musicians, bands, or orchestras" as your industry
  • 4. Enter SoundRat.com/{{user.userRoute}} as the "Business website" for this account
  • 5. Complete the form and link your account
Need help setting up your Stripe account? Contact hey@soundrat.com
